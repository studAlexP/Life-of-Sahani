Hey guys!

My name is Sahani. I´m 23, from Austria/Vienna with roots from Sri Lanka. I loooove being creative either old school with pen and paper or on my Computer. I've went to a few different school (I will not explain the school system in Austria now) but every school I went to after primary school had a main focus on some type of informatics. In the school where I got my a-levels, I learned a lot about web-design/photography/videography but also about editing videos and pictures. Which even more intrigued me to start making my own videos. I tried blogging in the past, I tried starting YouTube in the past, but I was never too interested in sticking to do that on a regular….soo I dropped it. But the pandemic and me failing my class during the first lockdown made me have a TON of time. Also I have a few people in my close friends group who also do YouTube and those people encouraged me even further to finally actually start. So I did. And that is how this journey started for me. I have to say I really have fun editing my videos and being as creative as I can be with them.
