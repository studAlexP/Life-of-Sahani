Creative Corner

I´ve can’t even tell you since when I was interested in art. I have pictures of me drawing when I was tiny, when I was a kindergarden kid, a teenager and I still draw when ever I feel like I wanna get away from all the stress and everything. It’s extremely calming and stress relieving for me, to just sit down and draw into my sketching book! I used to paint a lot with acrylic, but I don’t have much time for stuff like that anymore, that’s why nowadays I tend to stick to my pencil and my trusty sketch book. I don´t really have any kind of style I stick to at all times, I go through phases. But those phases seem to always come back. One thing I´ve always love, and I don’t know why, is drawing eyes. And if I can say so myself, I really improves in drawing eyes over the years. Other than that, I loooove graffiti and drawing portraits, although I’m still not a as good in drawing portraits as I would like to. But I will keep practicing!! If you´re interested in speed drawing with good music in the background, then you have to check out my video where I try to draw a picture which I’ve found on Pinterest!
