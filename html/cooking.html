Peach Curd Dessert

Ingredients

250g Quark/Curd
250g Heavy cream
100g Icing Sugar
1 pack Vanilla sugar
1 Lemon (organic would be best, you will need the lemon zest)
3 sheets Gelatine
1 tin Peaches

Curd Mixture

1. First you grab a big mixing bowl and mix the curd with the vanilla sugar and the icing sugar.
2. Then let the gelatine soak in cold water (this might differ in your case if you use another kind of gelatine)
3. Whip your heavy cream
4. For the gelatine, heat some water in a pan (about 100 millilitre)
5. While the water is heating up, cut your lemon in half. Take one half and grate the skin into the mixing bowl. Then squeeze the whole lemon
6. Dissolve the gelatine in the water you cooked and let it cool off a little
7. Pour in as much lemon juice as you would like, I like it on the sour side, so I used about three quarters of the lemon juice. But start off with less, you can always add!
8. Then pour in the gelatine and mix everything. The consistency is gonna be on the watery side
9. Next our whipped cream will be added. I always start off with a big dollop into the mixture so the whipped cream can be incorporated better. Then slowly fold the rest of the cream into the mixture.
10. Now you can taste test and add more lemon if you like
11. Then pour into a nice dish and put into the fridge. Best would be, to leave it in the fridge overnight.

TOPPING

This part of the recipe can be varied to your liking

1. Now for the topping, take the tin with the peaches and open it
2. Blend all of the peaches with a little bit of the sugar water that was in the tin
3. If your dessert is already hardened, just pour the blended peach juice over it and let it sit in the fridge for like an hour and you are good to go
