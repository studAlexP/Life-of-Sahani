Resident Evil

Resident Evil, SUCH a good game!!! My best friend got most resident evil parts for Christmas 2020 and firstly I watched him play with friends via Discord. But he soon after started to play them in chronological order for his YouTube channel, and I watched every part. At the moment he is done with Resident Evil Code Veronica and next up is Resident Evil Zero. As I’ve watched him play, I was really intrigued to play them myself. As of now, I enjoyed Resident Evil 2 the most and when I saw it was reduced to 15€, I had to buy it. Since I’m absolute shit at those type of games, even tho I love em, I thought it was probably funny to watch so I recorded myself playing it. As I predicted I really am shit at it and I was never this disoriented in my life xD

So that’s how I started my Resident Evil journey and I hope I will have more time to continue playing it when I´m done with this semester.

If you haven´t watched my RE videos, watch em! I hope you won’t cry watching me be lost af :’)
